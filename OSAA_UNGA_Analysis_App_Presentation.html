Application Overview

Mission Statement

The OSAA UNGA Analysis Application is an AI-powered platform designed to analyze United Nations General Assembly speeches, providing structured readouts, SDG mapping, and historical analysis capabilities for UN OSAA personnel.

Key Objectives

  • Automated Analysis: Transform raw speech content into structured UN-style readouts
  • Historical Context: Access and analyze 78+ years of UNGA speech data (1946-2024)
  • SDG Integration: Automatic detection and mapping of Sustainable Development Goals
  • Multi-language Support: Handle speeches in multiple languages with automatic translation
  • Interactive Exploration: Chat interface for deep-dive analysis and question answering

Target Users

  • UN OSAA analysts and researchers
  • UN Secretariat personnel
  • Member state representatives
  • Academic researchers studying UN discourse

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) System

What is RAG?

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines information retrieval with AI text generation, allowing the system to access and utilize external knowledge beyond the AI model's training data, resulting in more accurate and contextually relevant responses.
